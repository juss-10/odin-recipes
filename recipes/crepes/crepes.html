Crêpes

Description

Crêpes, or simply crepes, are a type of thin pancake originating from the Brittany region of France.

Ingredients

  • 300 g flour
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 50 g melted butter
  • 600 ml milk
  • 50 ml rum
  • 3 whole eggs

Steps

  1. Put the flour in a bowl and form a well.
  2. Add the eggs, sugar, oil, and butter.
  3. Gently mix with a whisk, adding the milk as you go. The resulting dough should have the consistency of a slightly thick liquid.
  4. Flavor with rum.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan and oil it very lightly with a paper towel. Pour a ladleful of batter into the pan, spread it out, and wait until it is cooked on one side before turning it over. Cook all the pancakes over low heat.
